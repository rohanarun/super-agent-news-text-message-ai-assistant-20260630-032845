Why people want AI by text

No new app to open

SMS-style interaction lowers friction. Recent launches like TXTDrop show growing demand for AI access through text messaging.

Fast, informal requests

People phrase requests naturally — “check this site”, “redo what we did yesterday”, or “follow up tomorrow”.

But chat alone isn’t enough

Many text-based assistants stop at replies. They don’t reliably complete multi-step computer workflows.
